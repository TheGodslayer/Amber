Jaden (Jade)

 

Name: Jaden Barnes (Jade)
Age: 19
Gender: Male
Type: Other

Description:
5'9. With an athletic build, weighing maybe170 pounds.
His hair is short, the color of stained wood, with dark eyes. Handsome, almost flawless.
He is quiet and loud at the same time, his favorite color is Black. There is something dark about him, but he seems outgoing and friendly.
He favors the latest styles and money never seem a consideration to him. He wears and discards things like Rolexes as casualy as he wears and discards his underwear. (Boxers.) The only limitations there seems to be in the clothes he wears is that they look good and feel comfortable.

Young and Cocky, his mouth definately gets him into trouble now and then. He never seems suprised by the outrageous or strange, but at time can be stunned by the common or ordinary.

The only oddaties he seems to carry with him are a collection of small steel balls, which he is never without and which seem impossible to number. They are the size of a marble or eye ball and Jade an often be seen toying with them of clinking them together.

Jade also often carrys a pack of tarro cards with him, kept in his jacket or shirt. He seems hesitant to show them to anyone, even those interested in such things.

History:
Not a lot is known about Jade. He recently arrived in Sunnydale to attend collage and lives on campus.
